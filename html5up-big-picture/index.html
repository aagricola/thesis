The Eyepillow

My first taste of creating alleviation for the mind and body was guiding someone to calm using a breathing technique and an eye pillow to drop inside and soothe the nervous system. Inhale for 4 exhale for 8.

nuBody(update)

There is a bed out of memory foam and adjustable pillows with flaxseed for weight and comfort. The headrest was stuffed with lavender to help with relaxation. It was necessary for a body to be lying on the bed in order for a meditation video lead by an aloe plant with the voice of Siri to begin to play on the screen.
